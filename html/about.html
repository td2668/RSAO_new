About the ORS


MRU's Research Services is here to help faculty develop their research and other scholarly pursuits. We help faculty find, apply for and administer grants – internal, external, or multi-institutional.

Explore the 'Research Handbook' for more information.

We:

  • locate funding sources and help you interpret and meet their requirements;
  • help you prepare proposals – planning, writing, budgeting and mentoring;
  • help you administer your grant – get you set up with accounting, remind you of renewals and help you with reporting;
  • coordinate institutional and multi-discipline applications and administer institutional grants;
  • act as the liaison with major funding agencies, keeping abreast and advising researchers of changes in programs as well as new directions and initiatives;
  • ensure compliance with MRU, agency and government regulations;
  • are the institution's window to the research community – promoting research success, putting the community in touch with experts, and facilitating community partner involvement;
  • maintain the research web site, database of opportunities and research discussion groups;
  • develop policies and procedures to support research and other scholarly activities;
  • keep abreast of research news and inform you of events on and off campus.
