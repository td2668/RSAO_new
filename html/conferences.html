Challenging Violence: Transforming Research Into Practice | May 16, 17, 2011

This multidisciplinary conference will focus on various topics of violence that affect the Canadian public. The aim is to bring service providers, academics, researchers, students and various professionals together to share knowledge about how research can inform practice: the practice of reducing, intervening and/or preventing forms of violence

Topics will include:
  • Violence in the workplace
  • School violence
  • Violence affecting seniors
  • Sexual violence
  • Relationship violence
  • Human rights violations

Keynote Speaker

Sally Armstrong is an Amnesty International award winner, a member of the Order of Canada, documentary filmmaker, teacher, author, human rights activist and contributing editor at Maclean's magazine.
