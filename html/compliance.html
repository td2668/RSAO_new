Compliance

Mount Royal University believes in maintaining high ethical standards when it comes to research and scholarly activities.  If you are an MRU researcher or if you are someone that wishes to conduct research here at MRU, you need to secure proper clearance for your research.

Step 1:
Mandatory for all activity Complete a Tracking and Signature Form. This is required for all activities, funded or unfunded -- even none of the following steps are necessary. MRU must maintain records of all research activities and whether they do or do not require specific clearances from ethics, biosafety, etc.
Step 2:
Mandatory for all activity Complete a Conflict of Interest declaration. If you have no conflicts, you'll just be ticking 'none'.
Step 3:
If you have animal or human subjects, complete the appropriate application.
Step 4:
If your research involves biohazards, complete the appropriate application.

If you require any of the latter compliance steps you'll have to limit your activities until the applications are appropved, so start early.
