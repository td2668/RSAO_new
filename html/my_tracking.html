Conflict of Interest

Public and professional confidence in the products of research depend upon trust in the integrity of the researcher. One important way of maintaining or enhancing this trust is to declare any types of relevant conflicts of interest and for an independent group to determine how to manage (or disallow) such conflicts. Any declarations made here will be brought to your Dean for a recommendation and to the 'Responsible Conduct of Research' Committee for a final decision.
