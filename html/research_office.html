Research Services

Research Services, located in U265, supports, manages and actively develops research activity at Mount Royal. We:

  • disseminate internal funding and provide information about external funds;
  • assist you in developing proposals and budgets;
  • administer research funds, handle all research-related financial transactions;
  • promote and celebrate research success;
  • keep abreast of changes policies and regulations and keep you informed;

Our Grants Facilitator, would be pleased to help you develop a proposal - whether for external or internal funding - no matter what the stage of your work. She specializes in helping faculty develop their research plan: a 3-5 year program involving multiple projects.

Our Vision

To encourage and support scholarship in all fields, particularly those that contribute to student learning, the student experience, interdisciplinarity and community responsiveness.

Our Mission

We will:

  1. Foster a culture of scholarship at Mount Royal.
  2. Focus on supporting a meaningful integration of scholarship and learning.
  3. Provide a sound and supportive research infrastructure that will maximize the effectiveness of funds dedicated to research projects.
  4. Dedicate ourselves to helping our faculty researchers and improving research services by meeting challenges through team efforts and creative problem solving.
  5. Provide appropriate incentives to researchers that will encourage student involvement as well as build external partnerships and attract funding.
  6. Foster effective and diverse cross- and multi-disciplinary efforts of faculty, staff, and students.
  7. Maintain the highest standards championed by the research administration profession to ensure the responsible conduct of research.
  8. Promote initiatives for external partnerships that will enhance the well-being of people in our communities.
