The Research Tracking Form

The MRU Research Tracking form is designed to provide the Office of Research Services with information we need to efficiently administer your research funds and to maintain our institutional legal and ethical obligations. If you wish to submit an application for research funding, you must first complete a Research Tracking Form.

Some departments require the submission of a Research Tracking Form for all research projects, funded or un-funded. For details, please contact your Dean.

Updates - Sept 2013

  • Electronic Approvals
    • The Tracking Form system has now been updated so that Dean approvals can be completed electronically.
    • When you submit a tracking form, your Dean will automatically be notified, and when your Dean has approved, the Research Office will be notified. At each stage you will receive an email so that you are aware of the outcome.
  • Ethics Tracking
    • When you indicate on your tracking form that your research requires REB approval, the Ethics Officer will be notified, and will link your REB application to your tracking form. This will allow you to log in to RSOnline and easily track the status of your ethics certifications, including expiry/renewal dates.
    • *NOTE: All REB applications, even those for research that is not associated with funding, will require that you complete a Research Tracking Form.

Tracking Forms Guide

Tracking Form Instructions - detailed instructions on tracking forms



How Do I Use the Tracking Form?

The Research Tracking Form may be found on the Office of Research Services website: http://research.mtroyal.ca

Sign into RSOnline

From the Office of Research Services homepage, the sign in screen may be accessed from the bottom of the left hand menu -- click 'Sign In'.

  • Use your regular Mount Royal University username and password to log in. This will give you access to the Research Dashboard.

The Tracking Form Table

From the Research Dashboard, choose "My Tracking Forms". From "My Tracking Forms" you can view the status of all forms you have submitted, view status of Research Ethics Board approvals, and initiate new Research Tracking Forms. To create a new Research Tracking Form, click "New Form".

Complete the Research Tracking Form

  • Info Tab
    • Use this tab to provide general information on your project
  • Funding Tab
    • If you are completing the Research Tracking Form to initiate a research funding proposal/application, complete this tab to provide details on that funding opportunity. If this Research Tracking Form is associated with a funding application, please click yes and complete the additional fields:
  • Commitments Tab
    • This tab allows your Dean and the Office of Research Services to review any institutional and administrative commitments that are required for the completion of your project. Please check the boxes beside any areas where you will require institutional commitments or administration.
  • COI Tab
    • Each research project that you undertake requires a formal declaration of conflict of interest. Please carefully read the instructions and descriptions of potential conflict areas, and answer as honestly and completely as possible. Once your project has been approved, if an unanticipated conflict arises, it is your responsibility to notify the research office as quickly as possible so that the conflict may be managed/mitigated.
  • Compliance Tab
    • In order to be in compliance with ethical standards around research and laws associated with potentially dangerous goods and situations, you are required to declare any certification requirements and any other potential hazards before you have begun your project. Please declare potential need for ethical or other approvals here.
  • Files Tab
    • Use this tab to upload any files associated with your project/grant application. At minimum, to review a research grant application, the research office requires the first few pages of the written proposal, including a list of all investigators, summary, and detailed description of the project, and the budget documents. Upload these documents in pdf format. If you have any any questions, please contact the research office.

Once you have completed all sections of the Research Tracking Form, click "Submit".

What Happens Next?

Once you have submitted your tracking form, your Dean and the Office of Research Services will be notified. Your Dean will be able to log in to the RSOnline system and review your proposal at the same time as the Research Office.
