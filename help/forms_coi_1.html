COI - Interest

If you or someone close to you could receive financial or other benefits from the research you are undertaking, the contract you are entering into, etc, then you should check this box. There are many possible methods for reducing the level of conflict, including an open call for contracts and handing off responsibility for deciding who is awarded.

Even if this interest has no influence at all on your research outcomes, an observer might not make that assumption, so declaring this interest is vital

In this, and in many of the possible situations below, simply declaring the conflict publically using this document serves to reduce its impact, as a reader can evaluate your work in light of the declared issue.
