Dissemination

Dissemination/Knowledge Mobilization and deliverables are crucial parts of any research project. At Mount Royal dissemination and/or knowledge mobilization can take many forms including presenting at conferences, publishing in academic journals, authoring books, reports to public bodies or community organizations etc. The applicant here should provide an estimate of what their dissemination/knowledge mobilization and deliverables plans are (i.e. what, where, when) with as much detail as possible.
